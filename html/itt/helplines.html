Hotlines (U.S. Only) A-Z

A

Addiction

American Addiction Centers(Alcohol): website

Call (888)-685-5770. This 24/7 addiction hotline is free & confidential for yourself or a loved one. They will listen to you without judgment, answer your questions, offer the support you need, and discuss your treatment options. Representatives work solely for AAC and will discuss whether an AAC facility may be an option for you. There is no obligation to enter into treatment.

Daylight Substance Abuse And Addiction Hotline: website

Call 1-877-566-3869. or Chat online. This 24/7, 365-days-a-year addiction hotline is free & confidential. They will listen to you without judgment, answer questions, share medical detox information and addiction treatment plan options catered to your needs. They understand that you never set out to become addicted to a substance, and that many people that become addicted to a substance desire to improve their lives

Recovery Village: website 1 (alcohol addiction), website 2 (drug addiction)

Call (352) -771-2700. This 24/7 365-days-a-year alcohol and drug addiction hotline is free & confidential for family members, friends, and those struggling with alcohol or drug addiction. Many of the helpline operators and facility employees are also in recovery, so they can empathize and help you or a loved one navigate this journey comfortably.

E

Eating Disorder

Crisis Text Line: website

Text HOME to 741741 to connect with a Crisis Counselor for free 24/7 support. A live, trained Crisis Counselor receives the text and responds, all from a secure online platform.

National Association of Anorexia Nervosa and Associated Disorders

Call 1-(888)-375-7767 Mon. - Fri. 9am - 9pm CST (Closed on Holidays). They are able to assist you if, you think/know you have an ED, if you think someone else has an ED, you want a referral, you want support and encouragement, o0r if you have general questions about eating disorders.

National Eating Disorder Associations (NEDA) Crisis Text Line: website

Text HOME to 741741 to be connected with a trained volunteer at Crisis Text Line. Crisis Text Line provides free, 24/7 support via text message to individuals who are struggling with mental health, including eating disorders, and are experiencing crisis situations.

National Eating Disorder Associations (NEDA): website

Call 1-800-931-2237 Mon. - Thur. 11am - 9pm ET and Friday 11am - 5pm ET (translation services offered) or Text (800)-931-2237 Monday — Thursday 3pm — 6pm ET and Friday 1pm — 5pm ET. Text and Call Lines Closed for holidays. Contact the NEDA Helpline for support, resources, and treatment options for yourself or a loved one who is struggling with an eating disorder. Helpline volunteers are trained to help you find the support and information you need. Please note that the Helpline is not a substitute for professional help. The helpline cannot provide any kind of advice or ongoing support. They are happy to listen to your concerns, provide information on eating disorders, help you locate professional help, and explore support options.

M

Mental Illness - General

National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI): website

Call 800-950-NAMI (6264) or text Helpline to 62640 Mon. - Fri. from 10am - 10pm ET The NAMI HelpLine is a free, nationwide peer-support service providing information, resource referrals and support to people living with a mental health condition, their family members and caregivers, mental health providers and the public. HelpLine staff and volunteers are experienced, well-trained and able to provide guidance.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: website

Call 988 or Chat Online for help. The Lifeline provides 24/7, free and confidential support for people in distress, prevention and crisis resources for you or your loved ones, and best practices for professionals in the United States. (Please note that the previous 1-800-273-TALK (8255) number will continue to function indefinitely).

Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA’s) National: website

Call 1-(800)-662-HELP (4357) or TTY:1-(800)-487-4889, SAMHSA’s National Helpline is a free, confidential, 24/7, 365-days-a-year treatment referral and information service (in English and Spanish) for individuals and families facing mental and/or substance use disorders.

P

Poison

National Poison Control Hotline: website

Call 1-(800)-222-1222 (Poison Control) right away if you suspect a poisoning. Help is also available online at webPOISONCONTROL®. Both options are free, expert, confidential and available 24/7 365-days-a-year.

S

Sexual Assault

If you have been sexually assaulted, it is important to know that this is not your fault. No matter the circumstances. Victims of sexual assault, child sexual abuse and incest are not to blame. Only the perpetrator is at fault. If someone is blaming you, don't listen to them.

Crisis Support Service(Northern Nevada and Eastern California): website

Call 1-(800)-330-0226 or Text SASS to 839863. This line is available 24/7, 365-days-a-year for immediate free and confidential support/assistance. Crisis Support Services are here to help all sexual assault survivors. It does not matter if the attack took place today or 30 years ago.

RAINN (National Sexual Assault Hotline): website

Call Call 800.656.HOPE (4673) or Chat online. This hotline is a free, confidential and available 24/7. Whether you’re looking for support, information, advice, or a referral, their trained support specialists are ready to help.

Planned ParentHood of the North Country New York Sexual Assault Services: website

Call 1-877-212 -2323. This hotline is available 24/7. The Sexual Assault Services (SAS) of Planned Parenthood of the North Country New York offers counseling, education and advocacy for survivors of sexual assault in Clinton, Essex and Franklin Counties. Whether the assault took place five minutes ago, or five years ago, SAS can provide hotline services, free and confidential counseling, support at the hospital or with law enforcement, information and referrals, and educational programs.

V

Veterans

Veterans Crisis Line: website

Call 988 and press 1 or Chat online or Text 838255 for free 24/7, 365-days-a-year confidential crisis support for Veterans, service members, National Guard and Reserve members and their loved ones. (You do not have to be enrolled in VA benefits or health care to connect).
